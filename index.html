M.K. BRAMER.

Hi hi! My name is MK Bramer! I am a student located in Cheney, WA, attending Eastern Washington University. My major is Visual Communication Design with a minor in Creative Writing! I chose the VCD major because I love the creative problem-solving aspect of the graphic design field, and I have been obsessed with visual media since before I can remember. From books, movies, TV, videogames, to comics and art galleries--if it’s visually interesting I’m all about it! My dream is to create book cover designs, as it combines both of my greatest passions in a fun and niche way!

I have 2 cats, Bucky & Goomba! I’ve had Bucky since I was 18, and me and my partner just got Goomba in November of 2023. Some other things I enjoy include goth music (music of any genre, really), drawing, video games, and camping!

I love design, I love getting to solve creative problems and come out with something I’m proud of by the end of the process!

Bye bye!
